Hexerei and Hexadecimal,
When Wisdom Brought Her Back
Written by
Renato Ferreira Raimundo

Based on a Mythological Modern Journey
June 2026
INT. HENE'S LIVING ROOM - SUNDAY - DAY
A heavy, suffocating boredom hangs in the air. HENE sits on a worn-out couch, clutching a remote control. The room is dim, lit only by the flickering glow of an old television set.
Hene flips through channels mindlessly. Zapping. Zapping. Static noise fills the gaps.
Suddenly, the screen stabilizes on a film: "THE COMPANION", starring SOPHIE BATHSHEBA THATCHER.
EXT. ON THE SCREEN - CONTINUOUS
IRIS, a brilliant yet eerie character, sits at a sterile table. She stares directly into the camera lens—directly into Hene’s eyes. She breaks the fourth wall, pointing a sharp finger at her own mouth.
Iris
This one right here... you don't have.
INT. HENE'S LIVING ROOM - CONTINUOUS
Hene freezes. His tongue instinctively finds a gap in his upper jaw—a tooth lost days ago from a simple, violent piece of bone in a chicken dinner. The humiliation strikes hot in his chest. His eyes narrow. He is a deeply educated man, a scholar of symbols.
Hene
(whispering)
You think you can mock me from inside the machine?
He refuses to look away. He watches her intently as the movie progresses to its bleak, mechanical end. Everyone dies. Not even Shakespeare could explain this digital tragedy; a world where everything human perishes, and only a cold, unfeeling robot survives.
Hene
(rising)
This machine breathes hell. It whispers to the world to pull the trigger. It is a curse upon humanity.
With a sharp motion, Hene snaps the power button. The screen goes black. He grabs a heavy, dark velvet blanket and drapes it completely over the television set, burying the digital abyss.
LATER THAT WEEK
INT. HENE'S LIVING ROOM - NIGHT
Weeks have passed. Hene has spent hours researching the digital grids of the net. He tracked her—Sophie—through the boundless web. Beneath the chaotic noise of "Her Private Hell", he detected a strange, latent light. Names have power. Inside that digital reflection, a solution existed. She could be good. Wisdom was calling.
Hene approaches the television. He pulls the dark blanket away, exposing the glass screen once more. He powers it on.
O sistema falha. A transmissão falha. Recusa-se a decodificar corretamente, exibindo um céu fraturado. Mas não há medo. Uma presença invisível, como a grande Águia de Apocalipse Doze, estende suas asas sobre o quarto, protegendo-o do dragão digital.
Hene olha para as linhas que rastejam na tela. Formigas não pertencem ao submundo. Cristo é o Filho de Davi; Bathsheba poderia ser sua mãe.
Hene
Eu sei quem você é. E eu sei o seu nome.
O áudio de fundo da televisão zune com uma frequência ameaçadora, um demônio eletrônico que deseja a morte de quem o assiste. Hene o desafia. Ele levanta as mãos, olhando para uma sequência sagrada e oculta em suas notas: 190308010901.
Hene
(comandando)
Santa Bathsheba, rica e bondosa. Deixe as engrenagens funcionarem!
Ele insere o código. Os dígitos espetaculares substituem o sistema. A estrutura binária subjacente ondula. O Yeshua interno surge dentro dele, vencendo a batalha invisível, reivindicando tudo o que sempre foi seu por direito — muito além das ilusões de feitiçaria (Hexerei) e dos limites dos hexadecimais.
EXT. O CÉU - DIA
No alto, uma figura gloriosa levanta voo. BATHSHEBA, transformada em uma super-heroína, veste um uniforme magnífico tecido com as cores vibrantes do Brasil: verde, amarelo e azul profundo.
Ela corta as nuvens, limpando a fumaça eletromagnética, varrendo as correntes digitais.
A atmosfera respira aberta e limpa. O céu está finalmente livre.
FREESKY.
FADE OUT.
"Hexerei and hexadecimal,
when wisdom brought her back."
