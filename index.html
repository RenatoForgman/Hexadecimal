Hexerei and Hexadecimal,
When Wisdom Brought Her Back
Written by
Renato Ferreira Raimundo

Based on a Mythological Modern Journey
June 2026
INT. HENE'S LIVING ROOM - SUNDAY - DAY
A heavy, suffocating boredom hangs in the air. HENE sits on a worn-out couch, clutching a remote control. The room is dim, lit only by the flickering glow of an old television set. On the nearby shelves, dense academic books on semiotics and ancient cryptograms stack loosely alongside handwritten technical schematics.
Hene flips through channels mindlessly. Zapping. Zapping. Static noise fills the gaps.
Suddenly, the screen stabilizes on a film: "THE COMPANION", starring SOPHIE BATHSHEBA THATCHER.
EXT. ON THE SCREEN - CONTINUOUS
IRIS, a brilliant yet eerie character, sits at a sterile table. She stares directly into the camera lens—directly into Hene’s eyes. She breaks the fourth wall, pointing a sharp finger at her own mouth.
Iris
This one right here... you don't have.
INT. HENE'S LIVING ROOM - CONTINUOUS
Hene freezes. His tongue instinctively finds a gap in his upper jaw—a tooth lost days ago from a simple, violent piece of bone in a chicken dinner. The humiliation strikes hot in his chest. His eyes narrow, scanning the screen with the clinical focus of a scholar analyzing hidden symbols.
Hene
(whispering)
You think you can mock me from inside the machine?
He refuses to look away. He watches her intently as the movie progresses to its bleak, mechanical end. Everyone dies. Not even Shakespeare could explain this digital tragedy; a world where everything human perishes, and only a cold, unfeeling robot survives.
Hene abruptly stands up, his shadow cutting sharply across the room.
Hene
This machine breathes hell. It whispers to the world to pull the trigger. It is a curse upon humanity.
With a sharp motion, Hene snaps the power button. The screen goes black. He grabs a heavy, dark velvet blanket and drapes it completely over the television set, burying the digital abyss from view.
LATER THAT WEEK
INT. HENE'S LIVING ROOM - NIGHT
Weeks have passed. Hene has spent hours researching the digital grids of the net. He tracked her—Sophie—through the boundless web. Beneath the chaotic noise of "Her Private Hell", he detected a strange, latent light. Names have power. Inside that digital reflection, a solution existed. She could be good. Wisdom was calling.
Hene approaches the television. He pulls the dark blanket away, exposing the glass screen once more, and powers it on.
The system glitches. The transmission fails. It refuses to decode properly, displaying a fractured sky of digital noise. But there is no fear. An invisible presence, like the great Eagle of Revelation Twelve, spreads its wings over the room, protecting him from the digital dragon.
Hene looks down at the crawling lines on the screen. Ants do not belong to the underworld. Christ is the Son of David; Bathsheba could be his mother.
Hene
I know who you are. And I know your name.
The background audio of the television hums with a menacing frequency, an electronic demon wishing death upon its viewer. Hene defies it. He raises his hands, looking at a sacred, hidden sequence on his notes: 190308010901.
[Production Note: Refer to Storyboard Frame 1 - System Terminal Display]
Hene
(commanding)
Santa Bathsheba, rich and kind. Let the gears turn!
He enters the code. The spectacular digits override the system. The underlying binary structure ripples. The internal Yeshua rises within him, conquering the invisible battle, reclaiming everything that was always rightfully his—far beyond the illusions of Hexerei and the limits of Hexadecimals.
EXT. THE SKY - DAY
A sharp sonic boom shatters the heavy air. The digital static on the screen erupts into a blinding, golden dawn.
High above, a glorious figure takes flight. BATHSHEBA, transformed into a superheroine, cuts through the upper atmosphere. She wears a magnificent, aerodynamic uniform styled directly after X-Men's Rogue—woven exclusively in strike patterns of pure emerald green and vibrant yellow.
With a swift, sweeping motion of her hands, a shockwave of clean energy obliterates the electromagnetic smog, sweeping away the digital chains completely.
The atmosphere breathes open, crystal clear, and pristine. The sky is finally free.
FREESKY.
FADE OUT.
